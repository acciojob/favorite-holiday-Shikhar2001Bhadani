Ooty


Ooty, officially known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India. It is renowned for its scenic beauty, pleasant climate, and lush greenery. Ooty is often referred to as the "Queen of Hill Stations." The town is famous for its tea plantations, gardens, lakes, and colonial architecture. Key attractions include Ooty Lake, the Botanical Gardens, the Rose Garden, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The town offers a perfect retreat for nature lovers and is a popular destination for tourists seeking tranquility and cool weather.
